# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This document specifies the requirements for Business-tier exclusive advanced analytics capabilities in Ovend. The Business tier (₦3,500/month) must provide substantially more powerful analytics than the Pro tier (₦1,500/month) to justify the 2.3x price premium. These advanced analytics will give Business tier vendors actionable business intelligence, competitive insights, and data-driven decision-making capabilities that Pro tier users do not have access to.

The advanced analytics will leverage existing data from the `store_analytics`, `orders`, `products`, and `users` tables without requiring new tracking infrastructure for the MVP. The features are designed to help serious vendors understand their business performance, identify growth opportunities, optimize product offerings, and make informed strategic decisions.

## Glossary

- **Analytics_System**: The overall analytics data collection, processing, and display system
- **Business_Dashboard**: The dashboard UI component that displays Business-tier analytics
- **Pro_Analytics**: The existing analytics capabilities available to Pro tier users (7-day summary cards, daily visits chart, daily revenue chart, top 5 products)
- **Business_Analytics**: The enhanced analytics capabilities exclusive to Business tier users
- **Time_Range_Selector**: UI component allowing users to select custom date ranges for analytics
- **Extended_Period**: Analytics data spanning 30 days, 90 days, or custom date ranges (vs Pro's 7-day limit)
- **Customer_Metric**: Analytics related to customer behavior (repeat rate, lifetime value, acquisition)
- **Product_Performance_Metric**: Detailed analytics about individual product and category performance
- **Conversion_Funnel**: The sequence of customer actions from visit to completed order
- **Comparative_Metric**: Analytics showing period-over-period changes (week-over-week, month-over-month, year-over-year)
- **Export_System**: Functionality to export analytics data in CSV, Excel, or PDF formats
- **Revenue_Forecast**: Predictive analytics projecting future revenue based on historical trends
- **Real_Time_Dashboard**: Live dashboard showing today's performance compared to previous periods
- **Geographic_Insight**: Analytics based on customer location data (city/state distribution)
- **Abandoned_Cart**: An order that was initiated but not completed by a customer
- **Customer_Lifetime_Value** (CLV): Total revenue generated by a customer over their entire relationship with the vendor
- **Average_Order_Value** (AOV): Mean transaction value calculated as total revenue divided by number of orders
- **Inventory_Velocity**: The rate at which products sell over time
- **Profit_Margin**: Revenue minus product cost, expressed as a percentage
- **Repeat_Customer_Rate**: Percentage of customers who have made more than one purchase
- **Cohort**: A group of customers grouped by a common characteristic (e.g., signup date, first purchase date)

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Extended Time Range Analytics

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor, I want to view analytics for 30-day, 90-day, and custom date ranges, so that I can analyze long-term trends and seasonal patterns that aren't visible in the 7-day Pro tier view.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a Time_Range_Selector with options for "7 days", "30 days", "90 days", and "Custom Range"
2. WHEN a Business tier vendor selects "30 days", THE Analytics_System SHALL retrieve and display analytics data for the last 30 days
3. WHEN a Business tier vendor selects "90 days", THE Analytics_System SHALL retrieve and display analytics data for the last 90 days
4. WHEN a Business tier vendor selects "Custom Range", THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a date picker allowing selection of start and end dates
5. WHEN a custom date range is selected, THE Analytics_System SHALL retrieve analytics data for the specified date range with a maximum span of 365 days
6. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display all summary cards (visits, orders, revenue, conversion rate) for the selected Extended_Period
7. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display daily trend charts for the selected Extended_Period
8. WHERE a Pro tier vendor attempts to access Extended_Period analytics, THE Analytics_System SHALL display only the 7-day view with an upgrade prompt

### Requirement 2: Customer Analytics Metrics

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor, I want detailed customer analytics including repeat customer rate, customer lifetime value, and average order value trends, so that I can understand customer loyalty and optimize my retention strategies.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display the Repeat_Customer_Rate as a percentage for the selected time period
2. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate Repeat_Customer_Rate as (number of customers with 2+ orders) divided by (total unique customers) times 100
3. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display the Customer_Lifetime_Value for customers acquired in the selected time period
4. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate Customer_Lifetime_Value as the sum of all order values for each unique customer phone number
5. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display the Average_Order_Value for the selected time period
6. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate Average_Order_Value as total revenue divided by total order count
7. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a trend chart showing how Average_Order_Value changes over time within the selected period
8. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display the number of new customers versus returning customers for the selected time period
9. THE Analytics_System SHALL identify returning customers by matching customer_phone across multiple orders
10. WHERE customer data is insufficient (less than 5 orders total), THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a message indicating that more data is needed for meaningful customer analytics

### Requirement 3: Product Performance Deep-Dive

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor, I want detailed product and category performance analytics including inventory velocity, low-performing product identification, and profit margin analysis, so that I can optimize my product mix and inventory decisions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a product performance table showing all products with sales data for the selected time period
2. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display for each product: name, units sold, total revenue, average days between sales (Inventory_Velocity), and sales trend indicator
3. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate Inventory_Velocity as the selected time period duration divided by units sold
4. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL allow sorting the product performance table by any column (name, units sold, revenue, velocity)
5. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL highlight low-performing products defined as products with zero sales in the last 30 days that were active during that period
6. WHERE the vendor has set product categories, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a category performance comparison showing revenue and order count by category
7. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a category breakdown chart visualizing the proportion of revenue from each product category
8. WHERE a product has a compare_at_price set, THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate discount percentage and display it in the product performance table
9. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display the top 10 products by revenue for the selected period (vs Pro tier's top 5)
10. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL allow filtering the product performance table by product category

### Requirement 4: Advanced Conversion Metrics

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor, I want detailed conversion funnel analysis showing drop-off rates at each stage from visit to completed order, so that I can identify and address bottlenecks in my sales process.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a Conversion_Funnel visualization showing the stages: Store Visits → Orders Initiated → Orders Completed
2. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate conversion rates between each funnel stage for the selected time period
3. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display the visit-to-order conversion rate (orders completed divided by store visits)
4. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display order completion rate (orders with status 'fulfilled' divided by total orders)
5. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display abandonment rate calculated as (orders not fulfilled divided by total orders) times 100
6. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL show the average time between order creation and fulfillment for completed orders
7. WHERE conversion rate is below 2%, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display an insight suggesting optimization strategies
8. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL compare current period conversion metrics with the previous equivalent period
9. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate period-over-period change as ((current period value - previous period value) divided by previous period value) times 100

### Requirement 5: Comparative Analytics and Growth Metrics

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor, I want week-over-week, month-over-month, and year-over-year comparisons for all key metrics, so that I can track my growth trajectory and identify trends.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display Comparative_Metric indicators for visits, orders, revenue, and conversion rate
2. WHEN the selected time range is 7 days, THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate week-over-week change comparing to the previous 7 days
3. WHEN the selected time range is 30 days, THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate month-over-month change comparing to the previous 30 days
4. WHEN the selected time range is 90 days or more, THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate quarter-over-quarter change comparing to the previous 90 days
5. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display percentage change with positive changes in green and negative changes in red
6. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display trend indicators (up arrow, down arrow, or neutral) alongside percentage changes
7. WHERE there is no data for the comparison period, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display "No comparison data available" instead of a percentage
8. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a growth summary card highlighting the highest growth metric in the selected period
9. THE Analytics_System SHALL identify the highest growth metric by comparing percentage changes across all tracked metrics
10. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display absolute value changes alongside percentage changes for all Comparative_Metrics

### Requirement 6: Revenue Forecasting

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor, I want revenue forecasting based on my historical performance, so that I can plan inventory, marketing spend, and business growth strategies.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHERE the vendor has at least 30 days of historical order data, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a Revenue_Forecast section
2. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate a 30-day revenue forecast using linear regression on the last 90 days of daily revenue data
3. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display the forecasted revenue for the next 30 days with a confidence indicator
4. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate forecast confidence as "High" when historical data spans 90+ days, "Medium" when 30-89 days, "Low" when less than 30 days
5. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a chart showing historical revenue alongside projected revenue
6. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL identify seasonal trends by comparing same-month performance across different years where data exists
7. WHERE seasonal trends are detected, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a seasonal indicator showing expected performance relative to average
8. THE Analytics_System SHALL define seasonal trends as consistent patterns where same-month revenue varies by more than 20% from annual average across multiple years
9. WHERE insufficient data exists for forecasting (less than 30 days), THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a message indicating more data is needed
10. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display the assumptions and data range used for the forecast calculation

### Requirement 7: Analytics Export Capabilities

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor, I want to export my analytics data in CSV, Excel, and PDF formats, so that I can perform deeper analysis, share reports with stakeholders, or maintain records.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display an export button with options for "Export as CSV", "Export as Excel", and "Export as PDF"
2. WHEN "Export as CSV" is selected, THE Export_System SHALL generate a CSV file containing all analytics data for the selected time period
3. THE Export_System SHALL include in the CSV export: date, visits, orders, revenue, conversion rate, top products, and customer metrics
4. WHEN "Export as Excel" is selected, THE Export_System SHALL generate an Excel file with multiple sheets: Summary, Daily Metrics, Product Performance, and Customer Analytics
5. WHEN "Export as PDF" is selected, THE Export_System SHALL generate a PDF report with formatted tables, charts, and summary insights
6. THE Export_System SHALL name exported files with the format "Ovend-Analytics-{StoreName}-{DateRange}-{ExportDate}.{extension}"
7. THE Export_System SHALL initiate file download immediately after generation completes
8. WHERE export generation fails, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display an error message and suggest retrying with a smaller date range
9. THE Export_System SHALL limit CSV and Excel exports to a maximum of 365 days of data to prevent performance issues
10. THE PDF export SHALL include the vendor's store name, logo, and the generation timestamp

### Requirement 8: Real-Time Performance Dashboard

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor, I want a real-time dashboard showing today's performance compared to yesterday and last week, so that I can monitor my business throughout the day and respond quickly to issues or opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a Real_Time_Dashboard section showing current day metrics
2. THE Real_Time_Dashboard SHALL display today's visits, orders, and revenue updated in real-time as new data arrives
3. THE Real_Time_Dashboard SHALL compare today's current values with the same time yesterday
4. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate same-time-yesterday comparison using data up to the current hour from the previous day
5. THE Real_Time_Dashboard SHALL display today's performance compared to the same weekday last week
6. THE Real_Time_Dashboard SHALL display the time of the last update
7. WHEN a new order is created, THE Analytics_System SHALL update the Real_Time_Dashboard within 5 seconds
8. THE Real_Time_Dashboard SHALL display a "pace indicator" showing whether today is on track to exceed or fall short of yesterday's performance
9. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate the pace indicator by projecting end-of-day totals based on current hour performance and comparing to yesterday's final totals
10. THE Real_Time_Dashboard SHALL display orders received in the last hour as a live activity indicator

### Requirement 9: Geographic Insights

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor, I want geographic analytics showing which cities and states generate the most orders, so that I can optimize delivery coverage, target marketing, and identify expansion opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHERE customer location data is available in orders, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a Geographic_Insight section
2. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display top 10 cities by order count for the selected time period
3. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display top 10 cities by revenue for the selected time period
4. THE Analytics_System SHALL extract city and state information from the customer_address field in orders
5. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a state-level breakdown showing order count and revenue by state
6. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display the percentage of orders from each geographic region
7. WHERE geographic data is missing or insufficient (less than 10 orders with location data), THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a message indicating more data is needed
8. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL allow filtering all analytics by a selected state or city
9. WHEN a geographic filter is applied, THE Analytics_System SHALL recalculate all metrics using only orders from the selected location
10. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL highlight the vendor's primary location state in the geographic breakdown

### Requirement 10: Enhanced Visual Analytics

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor, I want enhanced data visualizations including comparative charts, trend lines, and interactive tooltips, so that I can quickly identify patterns and insights without deep analysis.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display trend lines on all time-series charts showing the overall direction of the metric
2. THE Analytics_System SHALL calculate trend lines using linear regression on the displayed time period data
3. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display interactive tooltips on all charts showing detailed values when hovering over data points
4. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL use distinct color coding to differentiate between metrics: blue for visits, green for revenue, purple for orders
5. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display sparkline mini-charts next to key metrics showing quick visual trends
6. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a performance summary card highlighting best and worst performing days in the selected period
7. THE Analytics_System SHALL identify best and worst performing days by comparing daily revenue values
8. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display milestone indicators on charts for significant events (highest revenue day, most orders day, highest traffic day)
9. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL allow toggling between different chart types (bar, line, area) for time-series data
10. WHERE multiple metrics are displayed on the same chart, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL provide a legend and allow toggling visibility of individual metrics

### Requirement 11: Tier-Based Access Control

**User Story:** As a Pro tier vendor viewing analytics, I want to see preview indicators for Business tier features, so that I understand what additional insights are available if I upgrade, without feeling locked out of my current analytics.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHERE a Pro tier vendor accesses the analytics dashboard, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display all Pro_Analytics features without restrictions
2. WHERE a Pro tier vendor accesses the analytics dashboard, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display locked preview cards for Business_Analytics features
3. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display preview cards for: Extended Time Ranges, Customer Analytics, Product Deep-Dive, Revenue Forecasting, Export Capabilities, and Geographic Insights
4. THE preview cards SHALL include a feature title, brief description, sample visualization or mockup, and an "Upgrade to Business" call-to-action button
5. WHERE a Pro tier vendor attempts to access Business_Analytics features directly, THE Analytics_System SHALL redirect to the billing page with the Business tier pre-selected
6. WHERE a Business tier vendor accesses the analytics dashboard, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display all Business_Analytics features without any locked content
7. WHERE a Starter tier vendor accesses the analytics dashboard, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display basic summary cards with locked indicators for both Pro_Analytics and Business_Analytics
8. THE Analytics_System SHALL verify subscription tier status on every analytics page load by checking the user's subscription_tier column
9. WHERE a vendor's subscription expires or is downgraded, THE Analytics_System SHALL immediately restrict access to tier-specific features
10. THE Analytics_System SHALL log analytics feature access attempts with timestamp and subscription tier for usage monitoring

### Requirement 12: Performance and Scalability

**User Story:** As a Business tier vendor with extensive historical data, I want analytics to load quickly and remain performant even when querying large date ranges, so that I can efficiently analyze my business without delays.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Business tier vendor loads the Business_Dashboard, THE Analytics_System SHALL display summary metrics within 2 seconds
2. WHEN a Business tier vendor selects a 90-day time range, THE Analytics_System SHALL retrieve and display all analytics within 5 seconds
3. THE Analytics_System SHALL use database indexes on vendor_id, date, and status columns for efficient query performance
4. THE Analytics_System SHALL aggregate daily analytics data to reduce query complexity for extended time periods
5. WHERE a query exceeds 5 seconds, THE Business_Dashboard SHALL display a loading indicator with progress feedback
6. THE Analytics_System SHALL cache frequently accessed analytics data for 5 minutes to reduce database load
7. WHERE the cache is valid, THE Analytics_System SHALL serve cached data instead of querying the database
8. THE Business_Dashboard SHALL implement pagination for large data tables (product performance, geographic insights) with 25 items per page
9. THE Analytics_System SHALL limit real-time dashboard updates to once every 30 seconds to prevent excessive database queries
10. WHERE the vendor has more than 10,000 orders, THE Analytics_System SHALL use optimized aggregation queries that pre-calculate metrics at the database level
